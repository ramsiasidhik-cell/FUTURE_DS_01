# 📊 Business Sales Performance Analytics
### Future Interns – Data Science & Analytics | Task 1

---

## 📌 Task Overview
Analyzed business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, high-value categories, and regional performance — as part of the Future Interns Data Science & Analytics Internship (2026).

---

## 🗂️ Dataset
**Superstore Sales Dataset** – [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vivek468/superstore-dataset-final)

- 9,994 sales records across the United States
- Time period: 2014 – 2017
- Fields: Order Date, Region, Category, Product, Sales, Profit, Quantity

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **Microsoft Excel** – Data cleaning, pivot tables, analysis
- **HTML / CSS / Chart.js** – Interactive dashboard visualization

---

## 📈 Key Analyses Performed

| Analysis | Description |
|---|---|
| Revenue Trend | Year-over-year sales growth from 2014 to 2017 |
| Regional Performance | Sales and profit breakdown by 4 US regions |
| Category Analysis | Revenue split across Technology, Furniture, Office Supplies |
| KPI Tracking | Total Revenue, Total Profit, Top Region, Top Category |
| Insights & Recommendations | 5 actionable business recommendations |

---

## 📊 Key Findings

- 💰 **Total Revenue**: $2,297,201 across all regions (2014–2017)
- 📈 **Revenue Growth**: 51.4% increase from 2014 ($484K) to 2017 ($733K)
- 🏆 **Top Region**: West — $725,458 in sales, $108,418 in profit
- ⚡ **Top Category**: Technology — $836,154 in sales
- ⚠️ **Lowest Profit Region**: Central — only $39,706 in profit despite high sales

---

## 💡 Recommendations

1. **Prioritize West region** for marketing spend — highest ROI in both sales and profit
2. **Expand Technology product range** — top revenue category with strong margins
3. **Review Central region strategy** — discounting may be hurting profit margins
4. **Investigate Furniture margins** — high sales but relatively low profit
5. **Capitalize on growth momentum** — 51.4% revenue growth shows strong market position
